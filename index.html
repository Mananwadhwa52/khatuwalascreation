About Khatu Walas Creation

spa

"What began as a small prayer shared on Instagram has blossomed into a global sanctuary for divine attire."

From @khatuwalascreation to Your Altar

Khatu Walas Creation was born from a simple, heartfelt desire — to dress our deities in the finest silks and most intricate embroideries. Our journey started on Instagram, where we shared our first handmade Poshaks for Laddu Gopal.

The overwhelming love from the community of devotees inspired us to expand, creating this dedicated home for divine creations. Every stitch is an offering. Every bead is a prayer. We believe that dressing Radha Krishna is not just a ritual, but an intimate act of Shringar and devotion.
